Best Movies According To Brandi

3 of My Top 10 Movies

Breakin

Breakin is a childhood favorite of mine. It was my introduction to hip-hop. It embodies the culture; by displayin graffiti, rap, style, and break dancing which was not well recived by the masses in the early 80's. Breakin and movies alike paved the way for hip-hop culture and proved that this lifestyle would transcend trends and become a part of societal agriculture.

The Color Purple

The Color Purple is a movie that gives a voice to the victims of misgony, racism, colorism, sexual,and physical abuse during a white patriachal time in American culture. It shows the perseverence and tenacity that many black women used to survive and empower themselves during a gross period of human development.

Set It Off

Four women fed up with the injustices of living in a poor neighborhood; decide to seek revenge by robbing the surrounding banks in their city. Each woman is motivated by a differnt circumstance but use their bond in friendship to instegate justification for their actions.
